关于

The Aolike Refrigeration has been established in commercial refrigeration industry since 1998.

We provide our clients the one-stop cooling solution which covers design, development, manufacturing, supply, installation and customized services. 

Our product range covers every type from upright door coolers, to supermarket cabinets and promotional drink showcase open air coolers of different shapes and sizes.

Aolike refrigeration are approved through CE, ROHS and UL certifications. Hence, our reputation in the market and hard work allowed us to gain trust of such customers as Ab-Inbev, Pepsi, Kraft Food, General Mills etc.

The Aolike Group consists of four branches that serve our clients around the world: The Headquarters and factory are located in JiangSu, China (two hours away from Shanghai).

The Aolike regional office is based in Montreal, Canada; Kuala-Lumpur, Malaysia; Dubai, UAE.
